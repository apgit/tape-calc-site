About Calc Tape Calculator

Welcome! I am an independent software developer dedicated to building efficient, reliable, and accessible tools for professionals.

Our Mission

My journey in software development is driven by a passion for creating high-performance tools that solve real-world problems. Whether you are managing property finances or balancing daily business accounts, the Calc Tape Calculator suite is designed to streamline your workflow with the precision of a traditional adding machine, reimagined for the modern era.

Technology & Craftsmanship

I believe in building for the future. The Calc Tape Calculator suite is built using Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP) and Compose Multiplatform, ensuring a consistent and fluid user experience across both Android and iOS platforms.

  • Global Accessibility: Our applications support 33 languages, ensuring users worldwide can work in their native language.
  • Modern Standards: By transitioning legacy code to KMP, we provide faster, more stable updates.
  • Cross-Platform Reliability: Available on both the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store.
