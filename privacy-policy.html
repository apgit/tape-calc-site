4. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA 2026)

As of January 1, 2026, California residents have expanded rights regarding their personal information:

  • Right to Know/Access: You can request details about the categories of personal information we collect and share.
  • Right to Delete/Correct: You may request that we delete or correct inaccurate personal information.

You can exercise your right to opt-out via the "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link in our footer or through browser-based signals (GPC).
